JBDT Law Offices Website

For this website I worked closely with the staff at JBDT Law Offices to develop a visual look and feel that mirrored the vibe of their office. I researched photographers and set up an on-site photoshoot with the partners and staff, trying to pull out each's personality. Once the design was approved I worked with a developer to QA the final sites functionality.
